# CityCrew Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 26, 2026

## 1. Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- Name, username, and email address
- Profile picture and bio
- Current city and home country
- Languages spoken and travel interests
- Travel style preferences

### Location Data
- Your current city location (for finding local activities)
- Activity locations you create or attend
- Location data is updated manually by you, not tracked continuously

### Communications
- Direct messages and group chat messages you send
- Photos and videos you share in chats
- Activity discussions and comments

### Technical Data
- Device push notification tokens
- App usage data and timestamps
- Device platform (iOS/Android)

### Third-Party Data
- **Google Places API**: When you search for locations, your queries are sent to Google to provide place suggestions. Google's privacy policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To connect you with other travelers in your city
- To facilitate activities and meetups
- To enable chat and communication features
- To send push notifications about messages and activity updates
- To improve app functionality and user experience

## 3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:
- **Other Users**: Your profile, activities, and messages are visible to other app users as part of the social features
- **Google Places API**: Location searches are sent to Google for autocomplete functionality
- **Supabase**: Our infrastructure provider that hosts data (subject to their DPA)

## 4. Data Security

- All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/HTTPS
- Database access is protected with Row Level Security (RLS)
- Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device
- We do not use non-exempt encryption requiring export declaration

## 5. Your Rights

- **Access**: View your profile and data anytime in the app
- **Update**: Edit your profile information in Settings
- **Delete**: Delete your account and all associated data by contacting support
- **Opt-out**: Disable push notifications in Settings

## 6. Data Retention

- Active accounts: Data retained while your account is active
- Deleted accounts: Data permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- Messages: Retained until you or the conversation participants delete them

## 7. Children's Privacy

CityCrew is intended for users aged 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 17. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

## 8. Third-Party Services

- **Supabase**: Database and authentication hosting (https://supabase.com/privacy)
- **Google Places API**: Location search autocomplete (https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Expo Push Notifications**: Push notification delivery (https://expo.dev/privacy)

## 9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

## 10. GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights:
- Right to access your personal data
- Right to rectification of inaccurate data
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
- Right to withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@citycrew.app.

## 11. CCPA Rights (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
- Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
- Right to deletion of personal information
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@citycrew.app.

## 12. Cookies and Tracking

The CityCrew mobile app does not use cookies. We may collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the app experience.

## 13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

## 14. Contact Us

For privacy questions, data requests, or concerns, contact us at:
- **Email**: privacy@citycrew.app
- **In-App**: Settings > Help & Support
